Welcome to the Diaspora Aid Grant(DAG)

The Diaspora aid Grant (DAG) is a collaborative effort among Africans in the diaspora who have united to provide financial assistance to their counterparts back home. This initiative aims to support various projects and initiatives in Africa, addressing critical needs such as education, healthcare, infrastructure, and economic development.
