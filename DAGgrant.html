DAG grant

The DAG grant operates through a DAG grant system, which is the primary mechanism for providing financial assistance for African development. This grant is central to the DAG framework as it serves as the main avenue through which funds are channeled to support various initiatives and projects aimed at fostering development in Africa. The focus of the DAG grant is specifically on leveraging the resources and expertise of the African diaspora to contribute to the economic, social, and infrastructural advancement of the continent. By mobilizing financial resources from the diaspora community, the DAG grant plays a crucial role in addressing key development challenges and unlocking opportunities for sustainable growth across Africa..
